Raliance is a collaborative initiative dedicated to ending sexual violence in one generation, with a firm belief that sport plays a critical role as an avenue and platform in preventing sexual violence. Raliance believes that sport culture is uniquely positioned to set norms and standards that can create conditions in which sexual violence is not accepted and does not occur.
